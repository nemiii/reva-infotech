About Us

The story of Reva

Reva Infotech is a modern product and engineering company focused on building personal applications, AI-assisted tools, and custom software systems for ambitious teams.

Founded in 2015, we started with a simple belief: technology should help businesses move faster, make better decisions, and create better experiences. What began as a small team in Vadodara has grown into a 100+ strong team of passionate builders solving practical software problems.

Our name reflects how we work. Reva is about purposeful creation, thoughtful engineering, and building products that people genuinely enjoy using.
